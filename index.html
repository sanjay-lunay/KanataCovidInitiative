About Us


The Kanata Covid Initiative, formerly the Kanata Covid Food Drive is a youth-run charitable organisation with the mission of helping those affected during the pandemic. Our organization is affiliated with Rising Youth Canada, an organization that provides resources and funding to local youth lead community projects. In the past we have conducted two food drives to help our community, raising over 7,500 lbs of food and over $2,500 in donations. Recently, we have pivoted from food drives to host shelter drives in partnership with the Shepherds of Good Hope.
